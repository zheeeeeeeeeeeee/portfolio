Moving Health Off-Road Ambulance

Emergency Transportation for Everyone

Moving Health is an NGO in Ghana building off-road tricycle ambulances aimed to increase access to emergency healthcare in rural areas. Z's work for Moving Health focused on designing their next generation driver’s compartment. Past iterations have been limited by Ghana’s unique enviornment for manufacturing. The new design is laser cuttable and easily fabricated. Tangible results include a 2D and 3D model.

Continued work with Moving Health now focuses on electrifying the vehicles through the design of low-cost swappable batteries, swapping stations, and solar charging stations for off-road ambulances.
