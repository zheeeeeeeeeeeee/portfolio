Snap Turtle

First Attempt at Toy Design

Snap Turtle is a multiplayer reflex-testing game where players take turns reaching into the jaws of the snap turtle to retrieve game tokens (fish). The motorized turtle opens and shuts its mouth at random intervals, increasing in speed as the game. Snap Turtle is the final project for the MIT 2.00b Toy Design Class.
