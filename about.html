Zhixing Chen

Creative

Z is an artist, designer, engineer, and musician. Mainly, he is a creative with a passion for enhancing human experiences. Currently in his fourth year at MIT, he is pursuing a double major in Mechanical Engineering and Music. His work primarily focuses on product and industrial design, with a multidisciplinary approach that explores the intersection of technology, music, and dance. Dedicated to sustainability, Z seeks to apply his technical skills and imagination to make the world a better place. In his free time, he enjoys dancing, singing, songwriting, surfing, scuba diving, snowboarding, and traveling.
